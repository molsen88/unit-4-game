Image Collector!

How to play the game


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four images below. By clicking on an image you will add a specific amount of points to your total.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each image is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each image.
